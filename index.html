Amaturufu Card Game App

Amaturufu (pronounced: Ah-mah-too-roo-foo) is a popular card game played in Rwanda.
Almost every Rwandan has had some sort of exposure to the Amaturufu card game.
Thus, Amaturufu remains one of the main goto board games in many laid back Rwandan gatherings.
For the Rwandan diaspora, however, Amaturufu is more than just a game. For these Rwandans that live
far from their country and their childhood friends, Amaturufu carries with it a lot of rich memories.
